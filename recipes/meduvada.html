Medu Vada


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tsp chopped ginger
  • 2 chopped green chilies
  • 1 tsp black pepper (optional)
  • Few curry leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Soak urad dal for 4-5 hours and grind to a fluffy batter.
  2. Add chopped ingredients and salt to the batter.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Wet your hands and shape vadas with a hole in the center.
  4. Deep fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
