Dosa


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 3 cups rice
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for soaking and grinding
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 6 hours.
  2. Grind them into a smooth batter, mix together, and ferment overnight.
  3. Add salt and mix well.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and pour a ladle of batter, spreading it thin.
  5. Drizzle oil/ghee, cook until crispy. Flip if desired.
  6. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
