Mango Lassi


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ripe mango pulp (fresh or canned)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2â€“3 teaspoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • A pinch of cardamom powder (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add mango pulp, yogurt, milk, and sugar into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Add cardamom powder for flavor (optional).
  4. Serve chilled, optionally with ice cubes.
